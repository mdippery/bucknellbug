BucknellBug Help

Welcome to Bucknell Bug!

BucknellBug is an application for checking Bucknell University's weather feed, a service of the geography and geology departments. Upon launch, the application will check the weather feed, and continue checking the feed every fifteen minutes. Weather is listed in the application's Dock menu. Control- or right-click on the Dock icon to see the weather information, as shown to the right.

When the weather has been updated, the Dock icon will bounce once. Optionally, you may also use the Growl notification system (included with BucknellBug) to alert you to new weather.
