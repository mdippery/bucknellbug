BucknellBug Help

Welcome to Bucknell Bug!

BucknellBug is an application for checking Bucknell University’s weather feed, a service of the geography and geology departments. Upon launch, the application will check the weather feed, and continue checking the feed every fifteen minutes. Weather is listed in the application’s status menu, denoted by the cloud icon in the status bar. Click on the status icon to see the weather information, as shown to the right.

If you have enabled the Growl notification system (included with BucknellBug), a Growl notification will appear to alert you when new weather information is available.
